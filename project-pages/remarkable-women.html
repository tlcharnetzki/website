Project Details / Background
This piece was part of a series highlighting women in the community. After selecting Tiffany’s story from the applicants, I interviewed her and her husband and edited this segment. This story challenged me to show personal tragedy without being exploitative. Throughout the production, I ensured Adam and Tiffany understood the process and were comfortable during the interviews. For me, this story was about a husband who wanted to do something special for his wife and I hope that’s reflected in the final piece.
