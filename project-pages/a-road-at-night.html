Project Details / Background
I played a largely line production role on this project - coordinating interviews, shoots, and archival resources. Central to our approach was the commitment to telling Howard's complete story, steering away from a narrative solely centered on the accident. We aimed to capture the essence of his journey, blending personal and professional interviews to create a comprehensive and empathetic portrayal.
One of the project's key objectives was to spotlight the "brotherhood of sports." This translated into dedicating significant airtime to Howard's former teammates who continued to support him throughout medical challenges. My favorite part of this trailer is showing the team photo as preview to the interviewees today. It's subtle, but really communicates the balance we were hoping to strike.
